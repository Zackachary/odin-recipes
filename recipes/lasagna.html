Lasagna

Description

It's lasagna...

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ground beef
  • Ground pork
  • Mozarella
  • Ricotta

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375
  2. Lay lasagna noodles in bottom of pan
  3. Add tomato sauce, then ground meats and cheese
  4. Repeat steps 2 and 3 to create desired number of layers
  5. Bake for 35-45 minutes
  6. Let cool for 10 minutes before serving
