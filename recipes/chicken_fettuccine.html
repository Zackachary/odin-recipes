Chicken Fettuccine

Description

cheesy

Ingredients

  • Fettuccine
  • Garlic
  • Butter
  • Cream
  • Chicken, seasoned to taste
  • Parmesan

Steps

  1. Butter pan and cook chicken. Set aside
  2. Boil water and cook fettuccine
  3. While pasta is cooking, butter pan and brown garlic
  4. Add cream and parmesan, and mix to keep from burning
  5. When sauce is ready, pour over pasta and add chicken on top
